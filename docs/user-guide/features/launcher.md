# Using the Launcher

Open your favorite apps, files, folders, and websites in one click.

## Open it
**Features → Tools → Launcher**, or click its tile on Home.

## Add a shortcut
- **An app or file:** click **Add App / File**, pick it, give it a name, **OK**.
- **A folder:** click **Add Folder**, choose it, name it, **OK**.
- **A website:** click **Add URL**, type the address (e.g. `https://example.com`), then a name, **OK**.

## Open a shortcut
**Double-click** it in the list (or select it and click **Launch**). It opens with the normal Windows
app for that type.

## Edit or remove
- **Edit:** select an item, click **Edit**, change the name/target.
- **Remove:** select an item, click **Remove**, confirm **Yes**.

## Tips
- Your list is saved automatically and comes back next time you open the app.

## Troubleshooting
- **"Could not open…":** the file/folder may have moved or the link is wrong — use **Edit** to fix the target.

## Where your data is stored
`%APPDATA%\DigitalSecretary\data\launcher\shortcuts.json` (on your PC only).
